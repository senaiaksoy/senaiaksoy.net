Doctor Senai AKSOY

I provide consultation, examination, diagnosis and treatment in the following gynecological and obstetric areas:

  • Annual gynecological examination and pap smear,
  • Contraception methods
  • Menopause
  • Fertility, In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)
  • Prenatal Diagnosis
  • Pregnancy follow-up, obstetric ultrasounds and delivery
  • Medical termination of pregnancy (D/C)
  • Laparoscopic and endoscopic surgeries such as myomectomy, endometriosis and hysterectomy
